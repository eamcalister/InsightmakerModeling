Example 1.3.2. Insightmaker Automatic Unit Conversions.

In the Insight below we see that Insightmaker will automatically convert units of the same dimension, thus simplifying flow formulas. In this example \(y\) has units of feet, time has units of seconds, and \(m\) has units of inches per second. Setting the flow rate into \(y\) to equal \(m\text{,}\) the units are correctly converted; when \(m\) is \(12\) inches per second the slope of the simulated line is \(1\) foot per second.
